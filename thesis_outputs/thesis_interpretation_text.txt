STATISTICAL SIGNIFICANCE CODING
ns = p >= 0.05; * = p < 0.05; ** = p < 0.01; *** = p < 0.001

NOTE ON METHODS
Normality was assessed using the Shapiro-Wilk test. Pearson's correlation coefficient was used when both variables were normally distributed; otherwise, Spearman's rho was used. For H4, Welch's independent samples t-test was used when both groups were normally distributed; otherwise, the Mann-Whitney U test was used.

NOTE ON HANDGRIP
Dominant handgrip strength was approximated as the higher value from the right and left handgrip measurements because hand dominance was not explicitly available in the dataset.
